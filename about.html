About Poliform

Crafting contemporary elegance since 1970

Our Story

Poliform was founded in 1970, evolving from a small artisanal workshop into a global leader in contemporary interior design. With roots in Italian craftsmanship and an eye toward innovation, we've spent decades perfecting the art of creating living spaces that balance aesthetics with functionality.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design. Today, Poliform stands as a testament to the enduring power of thoughtfully designed spaces to transform how we live, work, and interact with our environments.

Our Values

Quality

Sustainability

Aesthetics

Dedication to clean lines, harmonious proportions, and timeless elegance.
